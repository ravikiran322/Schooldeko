About School Deko

School Deko is India's leading school discovery platform, helping parents find the perfect educational institution for their children. With over 25,000 schools listed across the country, we provide comprehensive information, expert guidance, and personalized recommendations to make your school selection process smooth and informed.

Our mission is to democratize access to quality education by providing transparent, reliable information about schools across India. Whether you're looking for a day school, boarding school, play school, or pre-university college, School Deko is your trusted partner in this important journey.
